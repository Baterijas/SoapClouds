The SoapClouds

This is a colorful cloud collection, inspired by the Polar Stratospheric Clouds also known as the mother of pearl. These clouds are often found downwind of mountain ranges. Their sheet-like forms slowly undulate and stretch as the waves evolve, featuring Pearl colors hence the name mother of pearl.
